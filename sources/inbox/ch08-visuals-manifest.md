## Topics I could NOT find a strong dedicated image for

- **Topic 4 (drop test), the actual drop/impact moment or a lab drop-tower rig.**
  I searched "drop test", "drop test rig laboratory", "drop weight impact tester",
  "package drop test box", and German "Fallprüfung" (returned zero relevant hits).
  Commons has plenty of aerospace/ordnance "drop test" photos (parachute drop tests,
  landing-gear drop-test animations only available as .gif/.webm, museum-displayed
  drop-test *vehicles*), but nothing showing a classic mechanical-engineering drop
  tower or the actual moment of impact. I used `ch08-drop-test-cargo.jpg` (test
  article being staged for an air-drop) as the closest honest substitute — see the
  caveat in that entry above.
- **Topic 6 (data acquisition / oscilloscope / sensor instrumentation on a test
  article), as a dedicated photo.** Searched "data acquisition test setup sensors",
  "oscilloscope wired test article", "strain gauge instrumentation wiring",
  "oscilloscope laboratory measurement", "accelerometer wired test article sensors",
  "structural test setup strain gauges wiring" — results were almost entirely archived
  PDF reports (Internet Archive scans with matching keywords in the OCR'd text) rather
  than photographs. I fell back to `ch08-shaker-console-instrumentation.jpg` (a
  technician at a motion-platform control console) as a weak, honestly-flagged
  substitute — see the caveat in that entry. If this topic is important, it may be
  worth revisiting with different search terms (e.g. specific instrument brand names,
  or German/French equivalents) in a follow-up pass.
